Wanderlust

ABOUT

As of yet there are few applications that truly motivate someone to travel. Beyond photos and videos, a person can only imagine what it would be like to go some-where. However, there's no better way to inspire a person to travel then to actually have them go and experience it themselves.

Our exhibit aims to go beyond the more traditional approaches of encouraging discovery towards a glimpse at the potential forms of travel planning. By bringing together aspects of the environment into one experience we create the user's trip even before they have decided to leave.

TECHNOLOGY

By creating stories through a tangible, immersive experience we will encourage discovery and exploration.

Arduino UNO

Computer hardware that interprets human interactions and returns the results of their interaction.

Potentiometers

User can look around a scene in both X & Y axis and using potentiometers we can map and measure their view.

Button

With a button rigged to the bottom of a walking stick we can detect when a user is walking. This allows us to move through the footage at an equal rate.

Rotary Encoder

A rotary encoder to emulate the action of pulling one self up. The encoder is attached to pully system and spins as the user interacts with the system.

Projection Hardware / Software

The experience uses 3 projectors and screens. This setup allows the user to be fully immersed in the environment.

Open-source Libraries

We've combined multiple open-source libraries to help bootstrap our development cycle.
